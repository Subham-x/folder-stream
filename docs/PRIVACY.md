# 🛡️ Privacy Policy

**Your files never leave your home.**

1. **100% Local:** Folder-Stream does not use the "Cloud." It runs entirely on your local machine and your local network (LAN).
2. **Zero Tracking:** No analytics, no cookies, no "home-calling."
3. **Manual Approval:** The server will ask you in the terminal before allowing *any* new device to see your files.
4. **Encryption:** Traffic is plain-text (HTTP). Use only on **trusted** home networks. Avoid public Wi-Fi.

**Verdict:** You are the owner, the host, and the gatekeeper.
